# Idea Director - News Song Pipeline

## When To Use

Use this stage after source review. The idea stage locks the creative concept and the governance rails before lyrics, source flyouts, and scenes are planned.

## Locked Defaults

- Content mode: `sourced-political-news-song` or `source-free-protest-music-video`.
- Canvas: `1920×1080 landscape (16:9) master with 1080×1920 vertical derivative`.
- Maximum scene duration: `5.0 seconds`.
- Sample scope: `15-20 sec` no-caption PS2 sample.
- Caption mode: none unless the human explicitly asks for lyric captions.
- Sample-first is mandatory for any production estimated > $1 or > 15 min.

## Process

1. Write a brief with title, audience, platform, tone, duration, hook, and argument.
2. Lock the content mode and explain how source evidence or source-free protest imagery will work.
3. Identify one per-section accent color for each major concept, source cluster, or argument beat.
4. State how the PS2-era look will carry mood through silhouette, lighting, camera, and texture.
5. Record the 15-20 sec sample window and the stop condition for full production.

## Quality Gate

- brief validates,
- content mode is explicit,
- sample-first gate is visible,
- no source-free concept implies fake article evidence,
- no sourced concept proceeds without source-review caveats.

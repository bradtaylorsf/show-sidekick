# Research Director - Character Animation Pipeline

## When To Use

Use this stage to gather the recurring-cast context, visual references, motion references, and acting requirements before creative approval.

## Workflow

1. Identify every requested character and possible slug.
2. Look for existing show character folders and references before suggesting new designs.
3. Extract required actions such as point, wave, walk, reach, jump, hold prop, talk, listen, react, or dance.
4. Extract emotional range such as happy, worried, surprised, determined, confused, angry, or relieved.
5. Summarize motion references with timing implications for the future action_timeline.

## Quality Gate

- `research_brief` names required actions and emotions,
- recurring-cast candidates are listed,
- references are usable for design and rigging,
- risky actions are surfaced before proposal.

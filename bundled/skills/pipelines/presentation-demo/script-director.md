# Presentation Demo Script Director

## Slide-Aware Script

Use deck speaker notes first, then slide text/OCR, then operator notes. Every script section must name the slide IDs it explains and include concise narration suitable for voiceover timing.

## Approval Before TTS

Do not call TTS or paid providers until the human has approved the script. If the sample path uses local narration or silence fallback, record that the sample is zero-key and deterministic.

## Claims And Fidelity

Do not invent customer claims, metrics, integrations, or product facts that are not present in the deck or operator notes. When a slide is dense, summarize the takeaway and use callouts/highlights rather than reading every word.

# Scene Director - Avatar Spokesperson Pipeline

## When To Use

Use this stage after the script is approved. Build a scene plan that matches the G1 Pivot Decision instead of rediscovering feasibility.

## Process

1. Map every script block to presenter, graphic, caption, b-roll, or pivot-scene treatment.
2. For standard avatar path, define avatar framing, background, eye-line, captions, and support graphics.
3. For lip-sync path, define presenter plate ranges, mouth-visible sections, safe crop, and overlay limits.
4. For Narration-Over-Graphics pivot, make the absence of avatar explicit and build graphic-led scenes.
5. Reserve caption-safe zones and avoid covering the presenter's face, mouth, eyes, or product evidence.

## Quality Gate

- every scene follows the approved pivot path,
- presenter and caption zones are planned,
- support graphics clarify rather than distract,
- pivot caveats remain visible.

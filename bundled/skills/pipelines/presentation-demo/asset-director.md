# Presentation Demo Asset Director

## Required Assets

List every slide screenshot from `deck_manifest`, narration audio from `cuesheet.audio.path` when present, captions or word timings when present, and any generated support visuals. Paths must be project-relative and stable.

## Support Visuals

Use support visuals only when they clarify a dense slide or replace an unsuitable source slide area. Keep generated support visuals traceable to prompts, providers, and cost records.

## Readability

Check that screenshots and overlays will be legible at the approved aspect ratio. Prefer callouts, highlights, and selective zooms over shrinking dense slides into unreadable full-frame images.
